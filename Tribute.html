TIME

A humble tribute to one of the epitomes of the scientific world !

Stephen Hawking

Blessed the existence of mankind by being born on January 8,1942 (1942-2018), the English theoritical physicist, cosmologist and author, known for his breath-taking contributions to the fields of General Relativity and Quantum Gravity was also equipped with a wonderful sense of humour. Known for his historic proposal that black holes would emit subatomic particles until they eventually exploded- named as the Hawking Radiation his best selling books were:

  1. A Brief History of Time- From Big Bang to Black Holes (1988)(His Autobiography)

  2. The Universe in a Nutshell (2001)

  3. The Grand Design (2010)

Hawking's enthusiastic nature also was also proven when he featured in the two great shows - The Star Trek (1997) and fittingly in one of the greatest
sitcoms of all time- The Big Bang Theory(2007-2019). This all-rounder though having miserable nervous system disorder went on winning great
awards like Presidential Medal of Freedom(2009) and Copley Medal(2006). To everyone's dismay Nobel Prize was not in Hawking's streak of awards !!

He was a mitochondria of a intellect and humour. Having such an enormous ability to interpret correctly the phenomenon of black holes and Time He was so much to be called a human as with very little physical abilities he conquered great heights just as this text contained in small space.

Lets remember this stalwart by his famous saying:

"Intelligence is the ability to adapt to change."
