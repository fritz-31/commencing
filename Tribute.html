A humble tribute to one of the epitomes of the scientific world !

STEPHEN HAWKING

Blessed the existence of mankind by being born on January 8,1942 , the English theoritical physicist, cosmologist and author,
known for his breath-taking contributions to the fields of General Relativity and Quantum Gravity was also equipped with a wonderful sense of humour.
Known for his historic proposal that black holes would emit subatomic particles until they eventually exploded- named as the Hawking Radiation
his best selling books were:

  1. A Brief History of Time- From Big Bang to Black Holes (1988)(His Autobiography)
  2. The Universe in a Nutshell (2001)
  3. The Grand Design (2010)

Hawking's enthusiastic nature also was also proven when he featured in the two great shows - The Star Trek (1997)
and fittingly in one of the greatest sitcoms of all time- The Big Bang Theory(2007-2019). This all-rounder though having miserable
nervous system disorder went on winning great awards like Presidential Medal of Freedom(2009) and Copley Medal(2006).

Lets remember this stalwart by his famous saying:

"Intelligence is the ability to adapt to change."

Salute to this Genius !!
